Registration — frequently asked

Quick, honest answers to what families ask most while registering a player. If something still isn't clear, your league's contact is listed on the event's landing page.

Nothing is charged — and nobody is registered — until you reach the payment step and confirm.
Everything before that is safe to explore, back out of, and redo.

Is my player registered before I pay?

No. You can move Back and Next through the steps as much as you like; the player is only registered once you confirm payment on the final step.

How do I read the progress bar at the top?

A finished step turns green with a check, and the step you're on is highlighted:

Player → 2. Details → 3. Waivers → 4. Pay

I closed the browser partway through — did I lose everything?

No. Your progress is kept as you go, so you can return and pick up where you left off. And since nothing is final until payment, an unfinished registration simply waits for you.

Why is the "Continue" button greyed out?

A step still needs something from you — a required field, or a waiver that hasn't been accepted yet. The step will point out what's missing; once it's handled, the button turns active:

Can I go back and fix an earlier step?

Yes — use Back anytime. A green check just means that step is complete; it doesn't lock anything. Nothing is committed until you confirm payment.

How do I register more than one player?

Finish this player all the way through, then start the wizard again for the next one. Each player is registered separately, so you always know exactly who's done.

What's the difference between the deposit and the full price?

The payment step shows the full price and, when the event allows it, a smaller deposit you can pay now to hold the spot — with the balance due later. If there's no deposit option, you'll pay the full amount.

Tip: if a price, a date, or a required field doesn't look right, don't guess — the league contact on the event's landing page would much rather answer a quick question than untangle it later.
