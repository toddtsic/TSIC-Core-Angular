Searching teams — frequently asked

Real answers for finding, reviewing, and managing whole registered teams — their placement, payment status, and club-rep contact.

Filters only take effect when you press Search.
The screen opens with a default search (Active teams) already run — change the filters, then Search again.

What do the different Owed badges mean?

A team's balance shows its payment health at a glance:

$250 owed Auto-pay scheduled Autopay failed

A plain amount means a balance is due; scheduled means an automatic payment is set for an upcoming date; autopay failed means a scheduled charge didn't go through and needs attention. The footer totals Paid and Owed across all results.

Why is there a small dot next to some team names?

That marks a team whose club rep has opted out of email — worth knowing before you try to reach them or send reminders.

Why are team names shown as "Club: Team"?

Teams display as Club: Team so you can tell clubs apart at a glance. The club rep's name, email, and phone are right in the grid, so you can reach the right person without opening the panel.

How do I move a team to a different age group or division?

Click the team name to open its detail panel, then use Move. Like Drop, it confirms before it acts — so a mis-click won't reshuffle a team by accident.

Why don't I see "Send Invoice Reminders"?

It appears only when some teams in your current results are payment-flagged. It emails those teams' club reps a reminder — skipping reps with no email or who've opted out — and tells you the count before sending.

Can I get these results into a spreadsheet?

Yes — Export downloads the current results to Excel, exactly as filtered.

Tip: to chase down balances, filter to a pay status, press Search, then use Send Invoice Reminders — it handles the whole flagged set at once and tells you how many reps it will actually reach.
