Team registration — frequently asked

Real answers to the things club reps actually get stuck on. If your question isn't here, your event's contact is listed on the event home page.

Adding a team to your Library is not the same as registering it.
This trips up more club reps than anything else — the first two questions below are the fix.

I added my team, but it's not in the event. What did I miss?

Your Library is a reusable roster of teams — saving one there does not enter it in this event. A team is registered only when you give it an age group for the event, using the blue Register button on the library team:

How can I tell, at a glance, which teams are actually in the event?

Watch which state each team is in:

In your Club Library → click Register → Registered for this event

Registered teams collect in the green Registered Teams card. If that card is empty, none of your teams are in the event yet — no matter how many sit in your library.

The Library drawer footer still says "Close." Is that a problem?

It's a signal. While the footer reads Close, you still have library teams that aren't registered. Once every team you intend to enter is registered, it changes to Done. Don't walk away on a Close if you meant to register more.

What exactly happens the moment I click Register?

You pick a Level of Play and an Age Group for this event, and the team is entered right then. That single action is the difference between "saved for later" and "playing."

My team came back with a "WL" badge — did something go wrong?

No. The age group you chose is full, so instead of turning the team away the system placed it on a WL waitlist. If a spot opens up, the team can be moved off it. You did nothing wrong.

I registered a team into the wrong age group. How do I fix it?

Age group is locked after registration, because it drives caps, waitlists, and fees. To correct it: remove the team from the event, then register it again with the right age group. The team stays safely in your library the whole time.

Can I rename a team? And should my club name go in the name?

You can rename a team in your library — but don't put your club's name in it. Schedules already show your club, so "Thunder" is right; "Riverside Thunder" reads as "Riverside Riverside Thunder" on the schedule.

There's no Delete option on one of my teams — only Archive. Why?

A team that already has event history can't be deleted, because that would erase the record. Instead you archive it, which retires it from view while keeping its history intact. Each team's menu explains why an action is unavailable.

When — and how — do I pay?

On the Payment step, which covers all your registered teams together. Depending on the event you can pay a deposit now to hold your spots, or the full balance. Teams still in your library (not registered) aren't billed.

Tip: before you close the browser, confirm every team you wanted is in the green Registered Teams card and the footer says Done. That one glance prevents the most common "my team never showed up" call.
