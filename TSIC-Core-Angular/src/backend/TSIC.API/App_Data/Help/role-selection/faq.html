Open a Registration — frequently asked

Quick answers for the screen where you pick which registration to open after signing in.

"No active registrations on this account" doesn't mean anything is broken.
It means this account isn't registered for any event yet. To register, go to the event's website and start a new registration there — see below.

Why am I asked to pick something right after I logged in?

Because one login can hold several registrations — different children, teams, or roles. This screen lets you choose which one to open. It's a normal step, not an error.

I only have one registration. Why do I still have to click it?

The screen never opens a registration for you — you always tap it yourself, so there's no doubt about which one you're in. One quick click and you're through.

I have several children (or teams). How do I tell the rows apart?

Look at the muted detail line under each event name. For a player it reads name • age group • team — that's how two of your children, or two teams, are distinguished on rows that would otherwise look alike.

It says "No active registrations on this account." What do I do?

That account simply isn't registered for any event. If you're trying to sign up, go to the event's website and start a new registration there — registering is done from the event, not from this screen. If you have a different account that is registered, use Logout and sign in with that one.

There's a search box instead of a list. Why?

When a role has many registrations, they're shown as a searchable dropdown rather than a long list of cards. Start typing part of the event, name, or team to filter down to the one you want.

I see a "Looking for a new event?" suggestion. What is that?

If you've registered for events before, we may suggest related ones you can join. It's optional — a shortcut to the event's website, where any new registration is completed.

How do I sign in as someone else?

Use the Logout button at the bottom of the screen, then sign in with the other account.

Tip: pick the registration that matches what you came to do — paying a specific child's balance, managing a team's roster, and so on. You can always come back here and open a different one.
