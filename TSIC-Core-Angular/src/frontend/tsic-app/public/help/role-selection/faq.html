Open a Registration

"No active registrations on this account" isn't a bug.

It means this account isn't registered for any event yet. To register, start a new registration on the event's website — see below.

Why do I have to pick something right after logging in?

One login can hold several registrations — different children, teams, or roles. This screen lets you choose which one to open.

I only have one registration. Why do I still click it?

The screen never opens one for you — you always tap it, so there's no doubt which one you're in.

I have several children or teams. How do I tell the rows apart?

Read the muted detail line under each event name. For a player it shows name • age group • team — that's what distinguishes rows that otherwise look alike.

It says "No active registrations on this account." What now?

This account isn't registered for any event. To sign up, start a new registration on the event's website — registering happens from the event, not this screen. If a different account is registered, use Logout and sign in with that one.

There's a search box instead of a list. Why?

When a role has many registrations, they appear as a searchable dropdown rather than a long list of cards. Type part of the event, name, or team to filter.

What is the "Looking for a new event?" suggestion?

If you've registered before, we may suggest related events. It's optional — a shortcut to the event's website, where a new registration is completed.

How do I sign in as someone else?

Use Logout at the bottom of the screen, then sign in with the other account.

Tip: pick the registration that matches your task — paying a child's balance, managing a roster, and so on. You can come back and open a different one anytime.
