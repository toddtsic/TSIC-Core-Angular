Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home base — the first screen you land on after signing in. It gives you an at-a-glance read on the event you're managing: key numbers, charts, status indicators, and current bulletins, all in one place, so you can see how things stand before diving into any one task.

What you'll see

  • Chart tiles — visual summaries like registrations or revenue over time.
  • Status tiles — quick indicators of where things stand right now.
  • Content panels — bulletins and other event information.

Widgets are grouped by category, with the quick-glance tiles up top and richer content below.

The Dashboard is yours to arrange.

Use the customize option to show or hide widgets and reorder them, so the metrics you care about are front and center. Your layout is saved to your account.

Customizing it

  1. Open customize from the header menu.
  2. Toggle widgets visible or hidden, and move them up or down to set the order.
  3. Save to keep your layout — or reset to defaults to start over.

Good to know

  • The Dashboard reflects the event and role you're currently in. Switch jobs or roles and it reloads for that context.
  • Your customizations are personal — they follow your login and don't change what other admins see.
