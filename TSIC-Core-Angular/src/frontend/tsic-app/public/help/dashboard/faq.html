Dashboard

This is your starting point, not a task screen.

The Dashboard summarizes the event at a glance. To act on anything, use the menus to open the specific tool — registrations, scheduling, communications, and so on.

Can I change what appears on my Dashboard?

Yes. Open customize from the header menu to show or hide individual widgets and reorder them. Save when you're done, and your layout sticks the next time you log in.

I hid a widget by mistake — how do I get it back?

Reopen customize and toggle it visible again, or use reset to defaults to restore the original layout in one step. Reset only affects your own Dashboard.

Do my changes affect other admins?

No. Dashboard customizations are tied to your login. Rearranging or hiding widgets changes only your view — everyone else keeps their own.

Why do the numbers change when I switch events?

The Dashboard always shows the event and role you're currently in. Switching jobs or roles reloads it for that context, so the metrics you see belong to whatever you've selected.

A widget looks empty or shows no data. Is something broken?

Usually it just means there's nothing to show yet for this event — for example, a registrations chart before anyone has signed up. As activity picks up, the tiles fill in.

Tip: pin the two or three numbers you check most often to the top and hide the rest. A lean Dashboard you actually read beats a crowded one you skim past.
