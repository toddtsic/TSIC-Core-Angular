Registering your club's teams

This wizard signs you in as a club representative and enters your teams into this event. Your event's name is shown in the banner at the top of every step so you always know which event you're registering for.

The one thing to get right

There are two separate actions, and it's easy to do the first and forget the second:

In your Club Library

A saved team record you can reuse every season. Not yet in this event.

Registered for this event

A library team you've given an age group for this event. This is what puts them on the field.

Adding a team to your Library saves it for reuse — but it does not enter it in the event. A team is only registered once you give it an age group for this event. If your Registered Teams card is empty, none of your teams are in the event yet — no matter how many are in your library.

The steps

  • Club Rep Info — sign in (or create a club-rep account) and review your club.
  • Waivers — if this event has a refund policy, read and accept it. (Skipped if it doesn't apply, or if you've already signed.)
  • Teams — build your library and register teams into the event (see below).
  • Payment — pay the deposit or full balance for your registered teams.
  • Review — confirm, then Finish.

On the Teams screen

  • Your saved teams live in the Library drawer on the right. Each unregistered team has a blue Register button — use it to pick a Level of Play and Age Group for this event. That's the moment the team is entered.
  • Registered teams appear in the green Registered Teams card. If the drawer's footer still says Close (not Done), you have library teams that aren't registered yet — don't walk away until the ones you want are in the green card.
  • A full age group places the team on a waitlist (shown with a WL badge) rather than turning it away.

Common mistakes

  • Adding to the library but not registering. The most common one — see above.
  • Putting your club's name in the team name. Don't — schedules already show your club, so "Thunder" is right, not "Riverside Thunder."
  • Changing an age group after registering. It's locked, because it drives caps, waitlists, and fees. To move a team, remove it and register it again.

If you get stuck

Each team's menu explains why an action is unavailable (for example, a team with event history must be archived rather than deleted). If something still isn't clear, the event's contact is listed on the event home page.
