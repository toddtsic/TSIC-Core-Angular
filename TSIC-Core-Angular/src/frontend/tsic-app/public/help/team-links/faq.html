Team Links

These links show up on player-facing team pages.

Anything you add here is visible to the families of the team you assign it to — so post only links you're happy for them to see.

Where do these links appear?

On your event's player-facing team pages. A link assigned to a specific team appears on that team's page; a link set to — All Teams — appears for everyone.

What's the difference between one team and All Teams?

Pick a single team for something that only concerns them (their practice schedule). Pick — All Teams — for event-wide resources everyone should have (the venue map, the event rules page). All-Teams links save you from adding the same link to every team one by one.

My link doesn't open, or goes to the wrong place. Why?

Almost always the URL. Edit the link and make sure it starts with https:// and is the complete address — copy it straight from your browser's address bar rather than typing it by hand.

I deleted a link by mistake — can I get it back?

There's no undo, but re-adding it takes seconds: click Add, pick the team, and re-enter the label and URL. It's worth keeping a note of important URLs somewhere outside the app.

Tip: before an event opens, click each link once yourself to confirm it lands where you expect. Families lose trust fast in a "Schedule" link that 404s.
