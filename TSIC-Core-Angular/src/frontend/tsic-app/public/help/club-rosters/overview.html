Club Rosters

This is your control panel for the teams you've registered. Pick a team to see its players, then adjust uniform numbers, move a player to another of your teams, or remove a registration.

Choosing a team

Use the Team dropdown at the top. Each entry shows the age group, team name, and how many players are on it. The first team is selected for you when the page opens; the Refresh button reloads everything if numbers look stale.

The roster grid

  • Player — click a name to open that player's panel on the right.
  • # — the uniform number, or a dash when one hasn't been set.
  • A red trash button removes that player's registration.

Click any column header to sort; the grid opens sorted by player name.

The player panel

Clicking a player opens a panel you can drag wider — its width is remembered. Inside you can:

Set the uniform number

Type a number in Uniform # and choose Save.

Move to another team

Pick a Destination team and choose Move. The list only shows your other teams.

Deleting a registration can't be undone.

The trash button asks you to confirm first — "Delete {player} from this team? This cannot be undone." Only the confirmation actually removes them. If you just want them on a different team, use Move instead.

Tip: moving a player keeps their registration — it just re-homes them to another of your teams. Reach for delete only when a player truly shouldn't be registered at all.
