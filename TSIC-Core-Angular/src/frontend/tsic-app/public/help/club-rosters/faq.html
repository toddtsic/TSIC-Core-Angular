Club Rosters — questions

How do I set or change a uniform number?

Click the player's name to open their panel, type the number in Uniform #, and choose Save. The grid updates right away. A dash in the # column just means no number has been set yet.

What's the difference between Move and Delete?

Move keeps the player registered and re-homes them to another of your teams. Delete removes their registration entirely and can't be undone. When in doubt, move.

Why can't I pick a destination team?

The Destination list shows only your other teams — never the one the player is already on. If it's empty, you only have the one team registered, so there's nowhere to move them yet. The Move button stays disabled until you've chosen a destination.

I deleted a player by mistake.

Deletion is final — there's no undo. The player would need to register again, or the event director can help restore the registration. That's why the trash button always asks you to confirm first.

A team shows no players, or the page says no teams were found.

"No players on this team" simply means nobody's been registered onto it yet. "No teams found for your club" means none of your teams have loaded — hit Refresh, and if it persists, check with the event director.

My change didn't take.

If a save, move, or delete fails you'll see a red message with the reason. Try again, and use Refresh to reload the latest roster before retrying.

Can I resize that side panel?

Yes — drag its edge to make it wider or narrower. Your chosen width is remembered the next time you open a player.
