Searching registrations

Find, review, contact, and manage the individual people registered in this event — players, club reps, coaches, and more. The screen opens with a default search (Active registrations) already run.

How it works

  1. Open Set Filters to choose what you're looking for.
  2. Click Search to apply them.
  3. Click a person's name in the results to open their detail panel.
Filters don't apply until you press Search.

If you close the filter drawer with unapplied changes, you'll be asked to run the search.

What you can filter by

  • Core — role, name, active status, pay status, payment method.
  • Hierarchy — check boxes down the League → Agegroup → Division → Team tree, or the Club tree, to scope to specific teams. Each node shows its team and player counts.
  • Contact & dates — email, phone, registered on-or-after a date.
  • Demographics — gender, position, grad year, grade, age range, school, club.
  • Billing — subscription status, invoice number.

Some filters only appear when the event uses that feature (RegSaver insurance, or ARB recurring billing). Note that a couple of them narrow the role for you automatically — an insurance filter, for example, implies you're looking at players.

Your active filters show as removable chips above the results, so you can always see — and clear — exactly what's applied.

Reading the results

  • Owed shows in red when a balance is due; a small calendar icon means an automatic payment is scheduled. The footer totals Paid and Owed across the whole result set.
  • Inactive registrations are styled with a strikethrough so they're easy to spot.

What you can do

  • View / edit — click a name to open the detail panel: update contact info and demographics, process a credit-card refund, or delete the registration.
  • Email — Email All, or check rows and Email Selected, to open the batch-email composer.
  • Invite — when your search is scoped to a single invitable role (Player or Club Rep), send a signed invitation to another event.
  • Export — download the current results to Excel.

Handy for collections

If the event uses recurring billing, "Look up CCs expiring this month" runs a live card-processor query and returns everyone with a card about to expire — deliberately ignoring your filters — so you can collect balances before an automatic payment fails.
