Registration Protection

Registration Protection (Regsaver) lets you insure a registration you've already paid for, so an eligible injury, illness, or other covered reason can get your fees refunded. This page adds it to players on your family account who aren't covered yet. It's underwritten by Vertical Insure.

How it works

Sign in Choose players Pay premium Covered
  • Sign in with your family account. If you're already signed in, this step is skipped.
  • Choose players — accept or decline protection for each eligible, not-yet-covered player.
  • Pay the premium — the quoted total for the players you accepted appears as Premium total; enter a card and choose Purchase Registration Protection.

What it costs

There's no flat fee — Vertical Insure quotes each player individually, and the accepted quotes are summed into the Premium total before you pay. You see the figure before committing.

"You're all covered"?

That message means every registered player on your account either already has protection or isn't eligible for this event — so there's nothing to buy today.

Your policy is emailed to you.

Once the purchase goes through, protection is active and Vertical Insure emails your policy confirmation shortly after.
