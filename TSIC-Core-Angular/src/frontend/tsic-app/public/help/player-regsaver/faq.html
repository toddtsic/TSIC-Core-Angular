Registration Protection — questions

What is Registration Protection / Regsaver?

It's insurance on your registration fees, provided by Vertical Insure. If a player can't participate for a covered reason, you can file a claim to recover the fees. This page is where you add it to players who aren't covered yet.

How much does it cost?

There's no fixed price — Vertical Insure quotes each player based on their registration. The accepted quotes add up to the Premium total shown before you pay, so you always see the amount first.

Can I cover just one of my players?

Yes. Accept protection for the players you want and decline the rest. You need at least one accepted to continue — if you decline everyone, the page reminds you to choose at least one or return to the event.

It says every player is already covered.

Then there's nothing to buy — each registered player on your account already has protection or isn't eligible for this event. You can return to the event home.

Why am I asked to sign in?

Protection is tied to the players on your family account, so you sign in with your family credentials to see them. If you're already signed in for this event, you skip straight to the offer. A "not a family account" message means those credentials aren't a family login.

How do I know it worked?

You'll see a "Protection purchased" confirmation, and Vertical Insure emails your policy details shortly after. If a purchase fails, the page shows the reason so you can try again.
