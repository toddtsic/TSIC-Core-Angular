Team Registration Protection

Team Registration Protection (Regsaver) insures the registration fees for the teams you've registered, so an eligible covered reason can get those fees refunded. This page adds it to any of your teams that aren't covered yet. It's underwritten by Vertical Insure.

How it works

Sign in Choose teams Pay premium Covered
  • Sign in with your club-rep account. If you're already signed in for this event, this step is skipped.
  • Choose teams — accept or decline protection for each team that doesn't have it yet.
  • Pay the premium — the quoted total for the teams you accepted shows as Premium total; enter a card and choose Purchase Registration Protection.

What it costs

Cost is per team — Vertical Insure quotes each team, and the accepted quotes add up to the Premium total you see before paying. There's no flat rate.

"Your teams are all covered"?

Every team you registered for this event already has protection or isn't eligible — so there's nothing to purchase today.

Your team policy is emailed to you.

After a successful purchase, team protection is active and Vertical Insure emails the policy confirmation shortly after.
