Team Registration Protection — questions

What does team protection cover?

It insures the registration fees for a team, provided by Vertical Insure. If a covered reason prevents participation, you can file a claim to recover those fees. This page adds it to teams that aren't covered yet.

How much does it cost?

It's quoted per team, so there's no fixed price. The teams you accept are summed into the Premium total shown before you pay.

Can I cover some teams but not others?

Yes — accept the teams you want and decline the rest. At least one team must be accepted to continue; if you decline them all, the page prompts you to pick at least one or head back to the event.

It says my teams are all covered.

Then there's nothing to buy — every team you registered already has protection or isn't eligible for this event.

Why sign in again?

Protection is tied to the teams under your club-rep account, so you sign in with those credentials to see them. If you're already signed in for this event you skip straight to the offer. "No team registration found for this event" means that account has no club-rep registration here.

How do I confirm it went through?

You'll see a confirmation that your team protection is active, and Vertical Insure emails the policy shortly after. If a purchase fails, the reason is shown so you can retry.
