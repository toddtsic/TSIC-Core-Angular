Camp Groups

Changes save as you make them.

Picking a group from a camper's dropdown saves immediately — there's no separate Save button. If a save fails, the value snaps back and you'll see an error.

What are day and night groups for?

They're the sub-groups a camp uses to organize campers — think rotation groups, cabins, or session assignments. A camper can have a day group, a night group, or both, depending on how your camp runs.

Why don't I see a Night (or Day) column?

A column only appears when that type of group is configured for the event. If Night is missing, night groups haven't been set up — so there's nothing to assign there yet.

How do I assign a lot of campers quickly?

Tick the campers (or use select-all on the visible list), pick a group in the bulk control, and apply. It sets that group on everyone selected in one go — much faster than row by row for a full cabin.

How do I find campers who still need a group?

Turn on unassigned only. It filters the list to campers missing a group in a visible column, so what's left on screen is exactly your remaining work.

Can I clear a camper's group?

Yes — set their dropdown back to the blank/none option and it saves as unassigned, which puts them back in the "unassigned only" view.

Tip: work one team at a time and finish it (use "unassigned only" to confirm zero remain) before moving on — it's the cleanest way to be sure no camper slipped through without a group.
