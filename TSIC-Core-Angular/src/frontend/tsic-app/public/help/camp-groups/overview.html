Camp Groups

Camp Groups is where you sort campers into their day and night groups — the sub-groups a camp uses for rotations, cabins, or sessions. Pick a team, then assign each camper to a group, one at a time or many at once.

How to assign

  1. Choose a team from the list to load its campers.
  2. Set a camper's Day Group and/or Night Group from the dropdown on their row — it saves the moment you pick.
  3. To do many at once, tick the campers you want, choose a group in the bulk control, and apply it to all of them together.
Use "unassigned only" to find who's left.

It hides campers who already have a group, so you can see at a glance who still needs placing.

Day and night columns

You'll only see the Day column if day groups are configured for this event, and likewise for Night. If a column is missing, that kind of group hasn't been set up — a camp that only runs day sessions simply won't show a night column.

Common mistakes

  • Assigning before selecting a team. Campers load per team — pick the team first.
  • Bulk-applying to the wrong people. The bulk action hits every checked camper. Glance at your selection before you apply.
  • Looking for a column that isn't there. No Night column means night groups aren't configured — that's a setup step, not a bug on this page.
