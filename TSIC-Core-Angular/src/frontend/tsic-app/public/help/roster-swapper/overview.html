Roster Swapper

Move players from one team to another. The screen is two side-by-side rosters — a Source Team on the left and a Target Team on the right. Pick a team on each side, select the players you want to move, and swap them across.

A swap is a real roster change.

The player is reassigned immediately — it affects their team, their schedule, and their coach's roster. Move the right player to the right team.

How to move players

  • Choose a team in the Source Team picker and another in Target Team.
  • Select one or more players in either roster, then use the swap control to send them to the other side.
  • It works both directions — Source → Target and Target → Source — so you can rebalance two teams without leaving the screen.
  • Selecting several players and swapping them together does the move in one batch.

Reading the roster count

Each panel shows a roster / max badge (e.g. 14/18) — current players over the team's cap. Watch it as you move players in so you don't push a team past its limit. Use the Filter players… box and the sortable columns to find someone quickly on a long roster.

The "Unassigned" pool

Staff and players who aren't on a team yet live in an Unassigned pool. Pick it as your source to place people onto teams, or as your target to pull someone off a team without deleting their registration.

Coaches and team requests

Coaches can request to be added to teams; those don't take effect until approved. The Coach Approvals queue (linked from the header) is where you review and approve them — the Requests and Role columns here show who's coaching what.

Common mistakes

  • Swapping the wrong direction. Confirm which side is Source and which is Target before you move — the button on each roster sends players toward the opposite panel.
  • Overfilling a team. Keep an eye on the roster/max badge; moving players in can exceed the cap.
  • Treating it like a draft board. Every swap is live — there's no separate "save," so each move is committed as you make it.
