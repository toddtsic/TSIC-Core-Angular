Roster Swapper

Every swap commits immediately — there's no separate save.

Each move reassigns the player right away, so double-check the player and the two teams before you swap.

Which way does a swap go?

Toward the other panel. The control on the Source Team roster sends selected players to the Target Team, and the control on the Target roster sends them back to the Source. Set both team pickers first so you're moving between the two you intend.

Can I move several players at once?

Yes — select multiple players in a roster and swap them together as a batch. Use the Filter players… box and the sortable columns to pick out the right people first.

What does the n/n badge on each team mean?

It's current roster over the team's maximum — e.g. 14/18 means 14 players on an 18-cap team. Watch it while moving players in so you don't overfill a team.

How do I get a player onto a team who isn't on one yet?

Pick the Unassigned pool as your source, select the person, and swap them to the target team. To take someone off a team without deleting their registration, do the reverse — swap them into the Unassigned pool.

A coach asked to join a team but isn't showing on the roster. Why?

Coach team requests have to be approved first. Open the Coach Approvals queue (linked from this screen's header) to review and approve them. The Requests and Role columns here show who's requested or assigned to coach each team.

I moved a player by mistake. How do I undo it?

There's no undo button — just swap them back. Set the team they landed on as the source, select the player, and send them to their original team. Because moves are live, correcting one is the same action in reverse.

Tip: when rebalancing two teams, put one as Source and the other as Target and work entirely between those two panels — you can move players each way without re-picking teams between swaps.
