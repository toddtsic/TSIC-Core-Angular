Update Payment Method

You're on a payment plan for this event — your balance is drafted automatically from a card we keep on file. This page lets you replace that card and settle the current balance in one step, so an expired or changed card doesn't cause a missed draft.

Your plan at a glance

At the top you'll see a summary of your plan: your name, the event, your subscription reference, and the current Balance Due. That's the amount this page will charge to the new card.

What you enter

Cardholder Information

First and last name, billing address, ZIP, and an email for the receipt.

Card Details

Card number, CVV, and the expiration month and year.

All fields are required, so the Update Card & Pay Balance button stays disabled until the form is complete. Choosing it saves the new card as your plan's card and charges the balance shown.

You'll get a clear result.

On success the page confirms your card was updated and shows the amount charged (with a transaction reference). If something goes wrong, it tells you the reason so you can retry.

Future drafts use the new card.

From here on, your plan draws from the card you just entered. This page doesn't change your plan's schedule or amounts — only the card behind it and today's balance.
