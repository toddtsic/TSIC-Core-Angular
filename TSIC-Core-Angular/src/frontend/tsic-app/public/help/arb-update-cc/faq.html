Update Payment Method — questions

Will this charge my card right now?

Yes — updating the card also pays the Balance Due shown at the top. If that figure is zero, no charge is made; the page just stores your new card for future drafts.

Does this change my payment schedule or amounts?

No. It only swaps the card on file and settles today's balance. The plan's timing and installment amounts stay exactly as they were.

Why is the Update button greyed out?

Every field is required. Fill in all of the cardholder and card details — name, address, ZIP, email, card number, CVV, and expiration month and year — and the button becomes active.

The page says it couldn't load my subscription.

The link may be invalid or expired, or it's missing its registration reference. Open it again from the Update CC Info item in your User Menu, or contact the event director for a fresh link.

The update failed — what now?

You'll see the reason on screen (for example, the card was declined). Double-check the card number, expiration, and CVV, then try again — or use a different card.

How do I get a receipt?

Enter the email you'd like the receipt sent to in the cardholder section. On success the page also shows the amount charged and a transaction reference for your records.
