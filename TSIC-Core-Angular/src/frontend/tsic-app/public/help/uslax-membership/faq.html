US Lax Membership Reconciliation

This is the bulk tool.

To check a single number without touching anyone's record, use the US Lax Membership Lookup instead. This page reconciles the whole roster and can email people.

Who shows up in the candidate list?

For Players, active lacrosse players with a membership ID on file. For Coaches, active unassigned adults with a USA Lacrosse number on file. Switching the role reloads the list for that group.

What does Reconcile All actually change?

It looks each membership up live at USA Lacrosse and updates the expiration date stored on the registrant. It doesn't change names, statuses, or anything else — those come from USA Lacrosse and are only displayed. The toast afterward tells you how many were checked and how many expiry dates changed.

Why is someone flagged when their status is "Active"?

Because their membership expires before this event's required date, or has no expiry on file. Active today isn't enough if it lapses before the event needs it — that's the same distinction the single-number lookup makes.

I selected 40 rows but the summary says fewer were sent. Why?

The server sends only to selected rows that both have an email and actually need action. Anyone already in good standing is skipped, and anyone without an email on file can't be reached. The send summary breaks down sent / failed / unsubscribed / no-email / skipped so you can see exactly what happened.

Can I customize the email?

Yes. The compose panel loads a default template (different for Players vs Coaches) with personalization tokens that fill in each recipient's details. Edit the subject and body freely — just keep the tone reporting status rather than accusing, since one message goes to people in several different situations.

How do I keep a record of the results?

Use the grid's Excel export to download the full reconciliation — every registrant, their status, involvement, and old/new expiry — for your files or to share with staff.

Tip: reconcile, then click select rows needing action — it checks exactly the flagged people for you, so you email the right list without hand-picking rows.
