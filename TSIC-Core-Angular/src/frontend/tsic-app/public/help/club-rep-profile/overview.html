Edit Your Profile

This is where you keep your club-rep contact details current — the email and phone the event uses to reach you, and your mailing address. Update anything that's changed and save.

What you can change

  • Your email and phone — the contact details the event and your teams reach you at.
  • Your street address, city, state, and ZIP.

Make your edits and choose Save Changes. You'll see a quick confirmation and land back on the home page. Back leaves without saving.

What's locked

A few fields show your current details but can't be edited here: Club Name, Username, password, and your name. They're greyed out on purpose.

Your name is fixed to protect the account.

Locking the name stops a club-rep account from being quietly handed to someone else by renaming it. If your legal name really needs to change, contact the event director.

Tip: keep your email and phone accurate — that's how the director sends you approvals, payment reminders, and event updates.
