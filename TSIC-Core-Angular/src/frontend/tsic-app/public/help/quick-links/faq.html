Quick Links

Families can't register? Check the switch here first.

These toggles decide whether registration and other paths appear on the public page — an "off" switch is the usual reason a button is missing.

What do these switches actually do?

Each one shows or hides a call-to-action on your event's public landing page — player registration, team registration, coach sign-up, store, and the like. On makes the button appear to visitors; off hides it.

Do I need to save after flipping a switch?

No — each toggle saves on its own the instant you flip it, and the change is live on the public page right away. There's no separate Save button, so treat every flip as immediate.

Why is one of the switches greyed out?

Either it doesn't apply to this job type, its fees aren't set up yet, or it's a SuperUser-only switch. The reason is shown right on the disabled toggle. Resolve that (correct job type, configure fees, or the right role) and it becomes editable.

A switch showed a warning when I turned it on. Did it fail?

No — the warning is a heads-up about a consequence (like opening coach registration before any teams exist), not a block. If that's intentional, leave it on; the switch still saved.

Are these the same settings as in Configure Job?

Yes. The same flags live in their proper Configure Job tabs; this screen just collects the public-facing ones in one place for speed. Change it here or there — it's the same setting.

Tip: when a registration window closes, come here and switch that path off. It's the cleanest way to stop new sign-ups without editing dates or fees.
