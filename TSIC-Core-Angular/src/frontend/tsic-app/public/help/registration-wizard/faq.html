Registration

Nothing is charged, and no one is registered, until you confirm payment.

Everything before that step is safe to explore, back out of, and redo.

Is my player registered before I pay?

No. Move Back and Next through the steps as much as you like — the player is registered only once you confirm payment on the final step.

How do I read the progress bar?

A finished step turns green with a check; the step you're on is highlighted:

Player → 2. Details → 3. Waivers → 4. Pay
I closed the browser partway through. Did I lose everything?

No. Progress is saved as you go, so you can return and pick up where you left off. Since nothing is final until payment, an unfinished registration just waits for you.

Why is the "Continue" button greyed out?

A step still needs something — a required field, or a waiver not yet accepted. The step points out what's missing; once it's handled, the button turns active:

Can I go back and fix an earlier step?

Yes — use Back anytime. A green check means the step is complete, not locked. Nothing is committed until you confirm payment.

How do I register more than one player?

Finish this player all the way through, then start the wizard again for the next. Each player is registered separately.

What's the difference between the deposit and the full price?

When the event allows it, the payment step offers a smaller deposit to hold the spot now, with the balance due later. If there's no deposit option, you pay the full amount.

Tip: if a price, date, or required field looks wrong, don't guess — the league contact on the event's landing page would rather answer a quick question than untangle it later.
