Registering your player

This wizard walks you through registering one player, one step at a time. You can move Back and Next freely — nothing is final until you reach the payment step and confirm.

What the steps look like

Your progress shows across the top of the wizard. A finished step turns green with a check; the step you're on is highlighted:

Player → 2. Details → 3. Waivers → 4. Pay

What you'll do

  • Choose the player — pick an existing family member or add a new one.
  • Fill in the player's details — the questions your league asks for this event.
  • Review the waivers — read and accept the required agreements.
  • Pay — see the full price, any deposit option, and confirm your payment.

When a step is complete and ready to move on, the Continue button becomes active:

Good to know

  • A green check on a step means it's complete. A step will tell you if something still needs your attention before you can continue.
  • Your progress is kept as you go. If you need to stop, you can return and pick up where you left off — the player isn't registered until payment is confirmed.
  • Registering more than one player? Complete this player first, then start the wizard again for the next one.

If you get stuck

Most steps show a short note explaining anything unusual. If a price, a date, or a required field isn't clear, your league's contact is listed on the event's landing page and is always happy to help.
