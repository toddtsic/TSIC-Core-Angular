Master Schedule

This is the whole event on one grid — every game, every field, laid out day by day. It's the definitive view everyone else works from: what you see here is what players, coaches, and referees see. Use it to review the finished schedule and to hand it out.

How to read it

  • Days run across the top. Pick a day tab to show that day's games.
  • Fields are the columns; times run down the side. Each cell is a game at that field and time.
  • The header counts the day's games and fields so you can gauge the load at a glance.
Big schedule? Use the minimap.

Press M for a bird's-eye navigator you can click to jump anywhere in the grid; press Esc to close it.

Exporting

You can download the schedule to Excel two ways:

Export Day

Just the day you're viewing — handy for a per-day field sheet.

Export Full

The entire event across all days in one workbook.

This is a view, not an editor

Master Schedule shows the schedule; it doesn't change it. To build or fix games, use the Schedule Hub, and for moving an individual game use the Rescheduler. Come back here to confirm the result and export.

If something looks wrong

A blank grid or a missing day usually means those games haven't been built or dated yet — check the Schedule Hub's Configure and Schedule steps. Before you publish or export for distribution, run QA to catch conflicts.
