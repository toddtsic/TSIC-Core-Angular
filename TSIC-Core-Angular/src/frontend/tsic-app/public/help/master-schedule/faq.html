Master Schedule

This screen shows the schedule — it doesn't edit it.

Build games in the Schedule Hub and move individual games in the Rescheduler; use this view to review and export the result.

How do I switch between days?

Use the day tabs across the top of the grid. Each tab shows one day; fields are the columns and times run down the left, so a cell is the game at that field and time.

Can I export just one day instead of the whole event?

Yes. Export Day downloads only the day you're currently viewing; Export Full downloads every day in a single Excel workbook. Both come out as .xlsx files.

The grid is huge and hard to navigate. Is there a faster way?

Press M to open the minimap — a scaled-down map of the whole grid. Click anywhere on it to jump there, and press Esc to close it.

A game is in the wrong slot. How do I fix it from here?

You don't fix it here — this is a read view. To move a single game use the Rescheduler; to rebuild or delete games use the Schedule Hub. Then return to Master Schedule to confirm and re-export.

A day is missing or the grid is blank. Why?

Master Schedule only shows games that have been built and dated. A blank grid or absent day usually means those games haven't been generated yet — check Configure and Schedule in the Schedule Hub.

Tip: run QA before you export a schedule for distribution. The exported workbook is exactly what people will play from, so it's worth confirming there are no conflicts first.
