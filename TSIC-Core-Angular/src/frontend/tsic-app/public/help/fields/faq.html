Manage Fields

A field must be in the League-Season panel to get games.

Assign every field you'll play on before building the schedule — the scheduler ignores anything still in the Available pool.

What's the difference between the two panels?

Available Fields is the shared pool of fields not yet in this event. League-Season Fields is the set active for this event's scheduling. Move a field from Available to League-Season to put it in play.

Why won't a field let me remove it?

It already has scheduled games on it — removing it would strand them. Move or clear those games first (in the Rescheduler or by rebuilding), then remove the field.

Can I assign several fields at once?

Yes — select multiple in the Available panel and assign them as a batch. The filter box and sortable columns help you pick the right ones from a large pool.

Where do the address and directions show up?

On the schedules families use to find the field. Open a field to set its name, address, map coordinates, and driving directions — complete details here prevent game-day "where is this field?" calls.

Is this the same as the Fields step inside the Schedule Hub?

It's the same tool. You can manage fields from the Schedule Hub's pipeline or from this standalone screen — either way you're assigning fields to the event's scheduling.

Tip: assign fields before pairings and timeslots. Fields are the foundation the whole schedule sits on — getting them in first keeps the rest of the pipeline from stalling.
