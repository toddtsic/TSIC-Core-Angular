The Store

The event store is where you buy merchandise and other items for this event — browse what's available, pick your options, add to your cart, and pay by card. Everything here is scoped to the current event.

Browsing and picking options

  • Each item shows a photo, name, price, and how many options it has.
  • Tap an item to open its options — Pick a Color, then Pick a Size. Sizes that are gone show as Sold out, and low stock reads "Only N left".
  • Use the quantity stepper to choose how many; it won't let you exceed what's in stock.

Buying for a specific player

If your family account has registered players, you'll pick who the item is for before adding it. That player's name then rides along with the item in your cart and on checkout.

Cart and checkout

Add to Cart Your Cart Checkout Order Confirmed

In Your Cart, adjust quantities or remove lines; the summary shows subtotal, any fees or tax, and the total. Proceed to Checkout, enter your card, and choose Place Order. You'll get an order confirmation with an order number and a Download Receipt button.

Signing in — or shopping as a guest

Shopping usually asks you to sign in with your family account. Where the event allows it, you can also Continue as Guest and check out without an account.

About "Stay-to-Play".

When your event uses it, Stay-to-Play in the menu opens this same store — it's just a differently-labeled way in. Any lodging items appear alongside the other store items.

Tip: stock is checked as you shop, so if an option shows "Sold out" or the count drops, it's live — pick another size or come back later.
