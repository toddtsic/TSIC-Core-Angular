Store — questions

Do I need an account to shop?

Usually you sign in with your family account. Some events also let you Continue as Guest and check out without one. If the store asks you to sign in first, that guest option — when available — is on the sign-in screen.

Why do I have to choose a player before adding an item?

When your account has registered players, the store asks who each item is for so it's tied to the right player. Pick the player and the name follows the item through your cart and checkout.

A size shows "Sold out" or "Only a few left."

Stock is live. Sold-out sizes are dimmed and can't be added, and low counts show how many remain. Choose another size or check back later.

How do I pay, and can I use American Express?

Checkout takes a credit card. Whether American Express is accepted depends on the event's payment settings — if it's not allowed, use another card.

What is "Stay-to-Play"?

It's an alternate menu label that opens the same event store. When your event uses it, any lodging options appear among the store items — there's no separate booking screen here.

Can I get a receipt?

Yes. The order confirmation shows your order number, total paid, and transaction reference, with a Download Receipt button for a PDF.

The store is empty or won't open.

"No items available right now" means nothing's currently for sale for this event. If the store is turned off entirely, it simply won't appear in your menu — check back closer to the event.
