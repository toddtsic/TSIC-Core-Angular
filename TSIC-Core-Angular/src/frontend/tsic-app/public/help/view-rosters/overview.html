Your Team Roster

This is your team's directory — every teammate and coach on the roster, each on their own card with the contact details we have on file. Use it to reach a teammate, a coach, or a parent in a couple of taps.

What's on each card

  • Name, and for players the uniform number, position, and graduation year when they've been set.
  • A coach / staff badge marks team officials. Players aren't badged — everyone else on the roster is a player.
  • The person's own email and phone, when they're on file.
  • A Family section with Contact 1 and Contact 2 — the parents or guardians on the player's account.

The and buttons are live: tap the envelope to start an email, or the phone to place a call or text from a mobile device.

Finding someone

Use the search box at the top to filter by name or position, and Sort to order the cards by name or by role.

Emailing the team

Tick the checkbox on any cards you want and choose Email Selected — or use Email All to message everyone on the roster at once. Your note goes to each person individually, so replies come back only to you.

This roster is shared with the whole team.

Every player, parent, and coach on the team can see these cards — including the phone numbers and email addresses. It's a team directory, not a private list.

Tip: on a phone, the email and phone buttons open your mail app or dialer directly — the fastest way to reach a coach before a game.
