Adult Registration

This wizard registers you for the event as an adult — a coach, referee, or recruiter. It walks you through signing in, filling out a short profile, accepting any waivers, and paying any fee. Your role comes from the link you followed, so the questions you're asked are tailored to it.

The steps

Account Profile Waivers Review Payment Done
  • Account — sign in, or create a new account. Coming back from the User Menu, you're signed in and dropped straight onto your profile.
  • Profile — the details for your role. Names are locked once your account exists (contact the director to change one).
  • Waivers — read and check each required agreement. This step is skipped when the event has none.
  • Review — one last look at everything, including any fees.
  • Payment — appears only when a fee is owed. Pay by card, or choose pay-by-check.
  • Done — your confirmation, with a button to resend the email.

Coaches: requesting teams

If you're registering as a coach, your profile may let you pick the teams you'd like to coach. This is a request only — it does not add you to a roster or grant roster access. A director reviews and approves coach assignments before you can see any roster.

Coaches: USA Lacrosse verification

Where the event asks for it, enter your USA Lacrosse number and choose Verify my USA Lacrosse membership. A 6-digit code is emailed to the address USA Lacrosse has on file; enter it to confirm. Can't reach that inbox? You can continue without verifying — a director will verify you before you're assigned.

If your card is declined, you're still registered.

Your registration is saved and a balance stays owing — no charge was made. Sign back in with the account you just created to pay it, or contact the event director.

"Registration Unavailable"?

That means the link you opened is missing its role. Ask the event director for the correct registration link for your role.
