Adult Registration — questions

Does picking teams add me to a roster?

No. Choosing teams as a coach is a request — it doesn't place you on a roster or give you roster access. A director reviews and approves coach assignments first. Until then you're treated as unassigned.

Do I have to verify my USA Lacrosse membership?

No — it's optional. If you can't get the emailed code, choose "continue without verifying" and a director will verify you before you're assigned. Verifying now just saves that step later.

The code never arrived.

It goes to the email USA Lacrosse has on file, not necessarily the one on your event account — the screen shows a masked hint of where it was sent. Check spam, or continue without verifying and let a director handle it.

My card was declined — am I registered?

Yes. Your registration is saved and no charge was made; a balance stays owing. Sign back in with the account you just created to pay it, or ask the event director for help.

Can I pay by check instead of card?

When a fee is owed, the Payment step offers Pay by Check. Your registration is recorded right away and confirmed once your mailed check is received.

Why can't I change my name?

Once your account exists, your name is locked on the profile step. To correct it, contact the tournament director.

It says "Registration Unavailable."

The link you opened is missing its role, so the wizard can't tell what you're registering as. Ask the event director for the correct registration link.

Why don't I see a Payment step?

Because there's no fee owed for your role at this event. When nothing's due, the wizard submits your registration straight from the Review step.

I didn't get my confirmation email.

On the final step, use Resend Confirmation Email. If it still doesn't arrive, check spam and confirm your email on the account is correct.
