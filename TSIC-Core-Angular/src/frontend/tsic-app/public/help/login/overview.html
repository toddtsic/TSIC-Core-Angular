Signing in

Sign in with your username and password to reach your registrations, teams, and account. One login can hold everything you're involved in — you'll choose which registration to open on the next screen.

What you'll enter

  • The top field is your username — this may or may not be an email address, depending on what you chose when the account was created.
  • Use the eye icon in the password box to show or hide what you're typing. Passwords are at least six characters.

What happens after you sign in

You'll usually land on a screen titled "Open a Registration". That's expected — it's where you pick which of your registrations to work in (see the help on that page). If you were headed to a specific place, we take you straight there instead.

Forgot your password?

Use the "Forgot your password?" link below the form. It sends a reset to the email on file for your account.

Tip: if a sign-in won't go through, check for Caps Lock and stray spaces first — they're the most common culprits. The FAQ tab walks through the rest.
