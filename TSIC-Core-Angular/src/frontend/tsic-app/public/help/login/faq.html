Signing in

"Invalid username or password" is deliberately vague.
For security it won't say which field was wrong. Check both — start with the username.
My password is right but it still says invalid.

The error covers both fields, so a wrong username looks identical to a wrong password. Check the username spelling, Caps Lock, and stray spaces. Still stuck? Use "Forgot your password?" to rule the password out.

Do I sign in with my email or a username?

The top field is your username. For some accounts that's an email address; for others it's a name you chose. Use whatever you set when the account was created.

The error message vanished while I was reading it.

It clears the moment you edit either field, so you can retry cleanly. That's not a sign the sign-in went through — you'll know it worked when the page moves on.

I forgot my password.

Click "Forgot your password?" and enter the email on file. The reset link goes to that address.

Is there a minimum password length?

Six characters. An older, shorter password won't be accepted — another reason to reset it.

After signing in I have to pick a registration. Is that right?

Yes. One login can hold several registrations — different children, teams, or roles — so "Open a Registration" lets you choose which one to work in.

I have more than one child or team. Do I need separate logins?

No. Sign in once; everything on the account appears together on the next screen.

Tip: still locked out after a reset? The event contact on the event's landing page can confirm the right username for your account.
