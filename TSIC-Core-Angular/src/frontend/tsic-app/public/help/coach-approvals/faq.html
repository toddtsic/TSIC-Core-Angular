Coach Approvals

A requested team is not access — approval is.

Coaches sit in this queue with pending requests until you grant them. Nothing they asked for takes effect on its own.

How do I actually approve a coach for a team?

In the coach's Teams cell, check the team they requested — that grant is immediate. Use Grant all to approve every team they asked for at once. To put them on a team they didn't request, use Add team.

What's the difference between a requested team and an assigned team?

A request is what the coach asked for (marked as coming from the coach); it's still pending. An assigned team is one they currently hold because you granted it. The Teams checklist shows granted teams on top and pending requests below.

How do I remove one team without removing the coach?

Uncheck just that team in the Teams cell — it removes only that grant. Don't touch Active for this; unchecking Active removes all of the coach's teams and drops them from the queue.

What does denying (unchecking Active) do?

It deactivates the coach: every team grant is removed and the coach drops off the worklist. It's the strongest action here, so it asks you to confirm. Use it when someone shouldn't be a coach on this event at all.

How do I know a coach is who they say they are?

Two signals. Coached before shows their prior team/job history so you can recognize a returning coach. On lacrosse events, the Verified badge shows whether they proved ownership of their USA Lacrosse membership email at sign-up, with the membership number and expiry; Re-validate refreshes that check.

The status chips at the top — what are they for?

They filter the list by Unassigned vs Assigned and carry live counts, so they double as a progress meter — how many coaches still need at least one team. Picking a chip pins the visible set; granting a coach updates the counts but leaves the row in place until you re-pick a chip or refresh.

Where do I move players and coaches between teams?

That's the Roster Swapper, which this queue is part of. This screen is specifically the approval gate for coach requests; the Swapper handles moving people between existing teams.

Tip: use the Unassigned chip as your worklist — it's every coach still waiting on a team. When that count reaches zero, everyone who registered has been reviewed.
