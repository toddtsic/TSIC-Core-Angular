Coach Approvals

This is the gate between "a coach signed up" and "a coach is on a team." Coaches register and request the teams they want to coach — but those requests do nothing until you approve them here. Each row is one coach; you decide which teams they actually get.

Granting a team gives a real adult access to that team.

Rosters, contact info, team messaging — approval is what unlocks it. Vet before you grant.

Reading a coach row

  • Status — the row's color shows whether a coach is Unassigned (holds no teams) or Assigned (holds at least one). The chips at the top filter by status and double as a live progress meter.
  • Coached before — a popover of the coach's prior team/job history, so you can recognize a returning coach.
  • Verified — for lacrosse events, shows whether the coach proved ownership of their USA Lacrosse membership email at sign-up, along with their membership number and expiry. Use Re-validate to refresh it.

Granting and removing teams

The Teams cell is the one control that does everything — it's a checklist:

  • Check a requested team to grant it. Grant all approves every team the coach asked for at once.
  • Add team places the coach on a team they didn't request — useful when you're assigning coaches yourself.
  • Uncheck a granted team to remove that one grant.

Acting on a coach recolors the row in place — it won't jump or disappear under your cursor, so you can work down the list steadily.

Denying a coach

Unchecking Active (or Deny) deactivates the coach entirely — it removes all their teams and drops them from the queue. It asks you to confirm first, because it's the strongest action on this screen.

Common mistakes

  • Granting without vetting. Check "Coached before" and, on lacrosse events, the Verified badge before approving someone you don't recognize.
  • Confusing "request" with "granted." A team the coach asked for is still just a request until you check it. Pending requests aren't access.
  • Denying when you meant to remove one team. Unchecking Active drops the whole coach; uncheck a single team in the Teams cell to remove just that one.
