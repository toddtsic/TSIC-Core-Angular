Searching teams

The team-level companion to Searching Registrations: find, review, and manage whole registered teams — their placement, payment status, and club-rep contact. The screen opens with a default search (Active teams) already run.

How it works

  1. Open Set Filters, choose your criteria, and click Search.
  2. Click a team name in the results to open its detail panel.

As on the registrations search, filters only take effect when you press Search — closing the drawer with unapplied changes will prompt you to run it.

What you can filter by

  • Waitlist / Scheduled status, active status, pay status, payment method, and level of play.
  • The same League and Club hierarchy trees — check down to specific agegroups, divisions, or teams.

Reading the Owed column

A team's balance tells you its payment health at a glance:

$250 owed Auto-pay scheduled Autopay failed
  • Plain amount — a balance is due.
  • Scheduled — an automatic payment is set for an upcoming date.
  • Autopay failed — a scheduled charge didn't go through and needs attention.

The footer totals Paid and Owed across all results.

What you can do

  • View / manage — click a team name to open its detail panel: edit the team's fields, Drop it from the event, or — when the event has more than one club — Move it to a different club (this reassigns the team to another club's rep). Both Drop and Move confirm before they act.
  • Export — download the current results to Excel.
  • Send Invoice Reminders — appears only when some teams are payment-flagged. It emails the club reps of those teams a payment reminder; reps with no email, or who've opted out, are skipped, and it tells you the count before sending.

Good to know

Team names show as Club: Team, and the club rep's name, email, and phone are right in the grid so you can reach the right person without opening the panel. A small dot by a team name means that club rep has opted out of email.
