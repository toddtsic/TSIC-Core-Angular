Email Log

A record of every email your event has sent — bulletins, batch messages, notifications. Use it to confirm a message actually went out, see who received it, and check when. It's your answer to "did that email send?"

Reading the log

  • The grid lists sent emails newest first, with Subject, Sender, Recipients, and the Sent time.
  • Sort or scan to find the message you're checking on.
  • Open a row to see its full recipient list — every address the message went to — which you can copy if you need it.
This is the "it did send" record.

If a message is in the log with a sent time and the right recipients, the system dispatched it.

What it doesn't do

The Email Log shows sent mail; it doesn't compose or resend. To send a new message, use Bulletins (for on-page announcements) or the batch-email tools; to see why a specific address didn't receive one, that's a deliverability question beyond this list.

Common questions this answers

  • "Did the reminder go out?" — find it by subject and check the Sent time.
  • "Who got it?" — open the row for the full recipient list.
  • "When was the last message sent?" — it's at the top; the log is newest-first.
