What this can and can't tell you

"Not blocked" means the address isn't on our email provider's block list, and a successful test means Amazon SES accepted the message — that's the most any sender can confirm, and it means the problem isn't on our end. It does not guarantee a message reached your inbox: it can still land in junk/spam, be held or silently dropped by your mail provider or security filter, or be rejected if the mailbox is full.
