# Ann's Punchlist - How to Use

## Adding an Item

Just tell Claude something like:

> "Add to my punchlist: [describe what happened]"

Claude will ask you a couple of quick questions if needed and log it for you.

## Reviewing Your List

> "Show me my punchlist"

## Marking Something as Fixed

> "Mark PL-005 as fixed"

That's it! Claude handles the formatting. You just describe what you found.
