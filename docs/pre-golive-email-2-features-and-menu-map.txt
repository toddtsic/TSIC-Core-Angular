Subject: TSIC NextGen Coming in August


Directors,

For those of you who have been testing the new system on dev.teamsportsinfo.com, I'm betting you've found you have no trouble locating and using your routine functions.

Today I wanted to give you a map of the new menu system for your reference, and introduce you to some of the new features.


Directors Menu Summary
(there can be per job per sports modifications):

  Search            Registrations, Teams

  Configure       Job Settings, Discount Codes, Age Ranges,
                         Quick Links (on/off switches for your Smart Bulletins)

  Teams & Rosters   L-A-D-T Editor, Roster Swapper, Pool Assignment,
                    Coach Approvals, Uniform Upload

  Scheduling        Schedule Hub, View Schedule, Bracket Seeds,
                    Master Schedule, Rescheduler, Tournament Parking,
                    Fields, Pairings, Timeslots, QA Results,
                    Mobile Scorers

  Communications    Bulletins, Email Log, Push Notification, Team Links

  Reports           Job Report Library (single location for all your reports)

(This layout is still in final testing, so a few labels or their placement may shift slightly before launch.)


New Features

HUGE Payment Options
  - E-Check is now accepted for any payment.
  - For tournament team registrations, ARB (Automated Recurring Billing) lets you take a deposit now and auto-charge the balance on a set future date.

HUGE Smart Bulletins 
  - Many bulletins you had to author and activate now write and activate themselves.
  - By using Configure > Quick Links you turn on/off registration (player, team, coach) and schedule visibility, whether job is offering registration insurance, whether public rosters are available and more.
  - Those settings feed the system what to present, so you no longer maintain those bulletins by hand.
  - They're role-aware -- the page and the top-right menu tailor themselves to who's logged in and where the job stands. A family sees "pay balance" or "manage my registration," a club rep sees "my teams," and the public sees links like "Schedule" and "Player Registration."
  - Some are purely informational. On a lacrosse job, an informational bulletin spells out the USA Lacrosse validation process and requirements right on the page -- before anyone registers -- heading off the questions your families would otherwise call about.
  - Use the bulletin system to talk to your audience, and leave the site wiring to Smart Bulletins.

HUGE Discount Codes
  - You can now offer discount codes to club reps registering teams.

Search Registrations
  - HUGE Send Invitations to specific user to register for a new event. You can have that future event CLOSED to the public, but link in invite email has security token that ALLOWS ONLY THAT PERSON TO REGISTER.
  - Can invite players to future player job, and if a tournament site, can invite a club rep to register for a future tournament.
  - Live Update link for any USA Lacrosse number (re-verifies and saves the expiration date)

L-A-D-T Editor
  - HUGE Early Bird Discount, Late Fees, now available for both player and team registrations

Register Teams
  - HUGE Club Team Library.  Using past registration history, we've built a per club team library. This now allows club reps to CHOOSE teams from their library rather than re-enter same team in multiple events.  
  - Club reps can archive old teams and add new teams as needed.
  - We can now assess a teams performance across multiple events.

Scheduling
  - Schedule Hub - one launch point for building and running
    your schedule.
  - HUGE Automated national-rankings download -- pull the current national rankings straight into your event.
  - Tournament Parking Tool -- a graphic and data view of the coming and going of people and cars, based on your schedule.

Reports
  - Job Report Library - every report for your job in one place,
    grouped by category with your recent runs kept on top.
  - HUGE Live report customization - several reports are now built on the
    fly instead of locked to a fixed layout. The Roster Table and
    Schedule List designers let you pick and reorder the columns, group
    and sort, set orientation, or start from a preset - with a live
    preview of the exact page before you generate the PDF.

That should get your ideas flowing!  Many jobs are already configured for the fall, but consider these new options for future jobs.

Remember, I'm available this month to do training.
